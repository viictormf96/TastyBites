Limonada de
Menta

A rich, creamy, and authentic Italian lasagna with layers of savory meat sauce, tender pasta, and perfectly melted cheese. A family favorite for generations.

Chef Sofia Rossi

schedule

Time

75 mins

bar_chart

Difficulty

Medium

local_fire_department

Calories

450 kcal

restaurant

Portions

6 Servings

restaurant Instructions

Prepare the Meat Sauce

In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef with diced onions and garlic. Drain excess fat. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian herbs. Simmer for 30 minutes to develop flavors.

Make the Ricotta Layer

In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese with one beaten egg, fresh parsley, and a pinch of nutmeg. Mix until smooth and well incorporated.

Assemble the Layers

Spread a layer of meat sauce in a 9x13 baking dish. Top with lasagna noodles, more sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers twice more, finishing with a thick layer of mozzarella and parmesan.

Bake to Perfection

Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown. Let rest for 15 minutes before serving.
